Our history

EMFORCO was born in 2025 from the will of historians and citizens to put the emphasis on forced conscription, while celebrating 80th anniversaries of WWII end.
Forced conscription has always been a polarizing topic among European people, but the current geopolitical environment forced us to act towards the recognition of forced conscription as one of the highest crimes during WWII.
We are enjoying an era of peace and fraternity that has shown its fragility. EMFORCO aims to reinforce people’s sovereignty and their right to decide their own fate by explaining, educating and advocating against forced conscription in Europe.
